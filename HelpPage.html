App Features

  • Add your schedule by pressing the settings cog on the top right of the home page.
  • View all your schedules for Days 1-7 by using the schedule button on the bottom tab bar.
  • View upcoming days using the calendar button on the bottom tab bar.
  • View house rankings with the trophy icon on the top left of the home page.
